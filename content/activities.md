## Bibliobattle

Tadahiro Taniguchi is the inventor of [Bibliobattle](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bibliobattle), a book review game that has spread widely across Japan and beyond. He serves as the Representative Director of the Bibliobattle Association.

- [Bibliobattle Association](https://www.bibliobattle.jp/) (Japanese)
- Received the Library of the Year 2012 Grand Prize

## AI Robot Association (AIRoA)

Founded and operating the [AI Robot Association (AIRoA)](https://airoa.or.jp/) since 2024, promoting AI and robotics research and industry collaboration.

## Moonshot R&D Program

Participating in Japan's Moonshot R&D Program, contributing to ambitious research goals in AI and robotics.

## Media & Outreach

- Author of multiple books on AI and robotics for general audiences
- Regular speaker at public events and conferences
- Columnist for various media outlets
